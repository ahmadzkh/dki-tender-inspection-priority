## Method

The transparent baseline uses train-split medians and robust scales to compute absolute robust z-scores for every feature. The final score is the mean of the top 5 feature deviations.

- Method: `robust_zscore_topk_mean`

- Score direction: higher score means higher inspection priority
- The score is an inspection-priority comparator, not a legal conclusion.

## Limitations

- Uses independent per-feature robust deviations; it does not learn multivariate interactions.
- Calibrated on 2024-2025 train rows; 2026 remains a partial snapshot.
- Ranking is a transparent comparator for inspection priority, not a violation label.
